Our Origin

Brewed in tradition, plated for tomorrow.

Founded in 2017, Fried Wings N Tea celebrates India's obsession with tea breaks and shareable snacks. We pair handcrafted beverages, crispy chicken, and hearty sandwiches inside vibrant lounges designed for all-day gatherings.

Purpose-driven sourcing

Estate-picked tea leaves, antibiotic-free poultry, freshly baked breads, and small-batch spice mixes keep every cup and wing consistent across the network.

Design-led lounges

Chai theatre counters, open fry stations, and community seating keep friends, families, and office crews coming back for their favourite brews and bites.

Technology-first operations

Integrated POS, beverage batching, and centralised marination ensure speed, quality, and profitability across dine-in, takeaway, and delivery.

Guest Journey

Immersive lounge experience engineered for repeat visits.

  • Brewing theatre, thickshake lab, and live wing fry line keep the action centre stage.
